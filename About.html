About Us

            In Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba, Hashira are the highest-ranking Demon Slayers, 
            known for their exceptional strength and skill in combat.
            To become a Hashira, a Demon Slayer must either defeat 50 demons or defeat a Twelve Kizuki member. 
            They are considered the pillars of the Demon Slayer Corps, 
            protecting humanity from the most dangerous threats.

              Hashira are the highest-ranking Demon Slayers in the Demon Slayer Corps, 
              renowned for their exceptional skills and mastery of Breathing Styles, 
              which are unique forms of swordsmanship. 
              They protect other Corps members and are considered the strongest within the organization. 
              Each Hashira specializes in a particular Breathing Style, 
              such as Water, Flame, Wind, Mist, Stone, and Sound, among others.
